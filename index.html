Customer Churn Prediction for Telecom Sector

Telecom customer data was analyzed to understand who is likely to churn. Logistic regression produced 83% accuracy, with churn risk highest among month-to-month contract users and those with high monthly charges. These results suggest focusing retention efforts on flexible plan customers to reduce attrition.
